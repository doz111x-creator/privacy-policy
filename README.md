# privacy-policy Effective date: May 8, 2026

Smart Clock & Timer (“the app”) is provided by Woxev.

This app is designed to help users manage timers, alarms, and clock-related settings on their Android devices.

Information the app accesses

Timer names, categories, durations, and alarm labels entered by the user
Audio files selected by the user for alarms and timers
Image files selected by the user for alarms and timers
App settings such as theme color, notification repeat options, and volume preferences
How information is used

To save and display timers and alarms
To play selected sounds for alarms and timers
To display selected images inside the app
To send notifications and exact alarms requested by the user
Data collection and sharing

The app does not collect personal data to remote servers
The app does not sell personal data
The app does not share personal data with third parties
The app does not use advertising SDKs
Data storage
All timer, alarm, and settings data is stored locally on the user’s device. Selected media files remain on the device and are only accessed for app functionality.

Permissions
The app may request permissions such as notifications and exact alarms in order to provide its core features.

User control
Users can edit or delete their timers, alarms, and settings at any time by using the app or uninstalling it.

Children’s privacy
This app is not specifically directed to children.

Changes to this policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updated version should be published with the app listing.
